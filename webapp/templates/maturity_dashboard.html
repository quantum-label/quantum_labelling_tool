The Maturity Label and the Data Quality & Utility Label are two distinct and independent concepts.

  • The Maturity Label evaluates the organisational maturity of the data holder (e.g., governance, processes, infrastructure).
  • The Data Quality & Utility Label evaluates the quality and utility of an individual dataset.

Neither label is derived from the other — they complement each other by providing different perspectives: one at the organizational level, the other at the dataset level.
